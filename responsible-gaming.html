Signs of Problem Gambling

If you or someone you know experiences:

Difficulty controlling time or money spent on gaming

Neglecting responsibilities due to gambling

Borrowing money or chasing losses

Feelings of stress, anxiety, or depression related to gaming

It may be time to seek help.

Getting Help

Professional resources are available to support individuals and families affected by problem gambling. You can contact:

Manitoba Problem Gambling Help Line: 1-800-463-1554

Canadian Problem Gambling Helpline: 1-888-230-3505

Gambling Support Services: Available through local health providers and community organizations
