Signs of Problem Gambling

It's important to recognize the warning signs of problem gambling:

  • Spending more time and money on gambling than intended
  • Neglecting work, family, or other responsibilities
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to gamble
  • Feeling anxious, depressed, or irritable when not gambling
  • Lying to family and friends about gambling activities
  • Chasing losses by gambling more to win back money
  • Experiencing financial problems due to gambling
