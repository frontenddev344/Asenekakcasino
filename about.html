About Aseneskak Casino

Aseneskak Casino is an Indigenous-owned and operated gaming facility located in Opaskwayak Cree Nation, just north of The Pas, Manitoba. Established through a partnership of six First Nations communities, the casino serves as both an entertainment destination and an important economic driver for the region.

Our mission is to provide a safe, respectful, and enjoyable environment for guests and staff alike. We take pride in blending modern gaming amenities with cultural values, supporting community development while offering a first-class experience for visitors and locals.
